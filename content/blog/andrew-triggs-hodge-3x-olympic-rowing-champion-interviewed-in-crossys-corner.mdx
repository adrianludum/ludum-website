One of Great Britain’s most decorated oarsmen, Andrew Triggs Hodge has won Olympic Rowing Titles at the 2008, 2012 and 2016 Olympics as well as four World Championship Titles. 

In this Interview with Martin Cross, he and Andrew look back on his career as well as discussing more topical subjects such as the announcement that lightweight rowing will be included in the 2024 Olympic Games and about the work that Andrew is doing with [London Youth Rowing](https://www.londonyouthrowing.com/) and [Race the Thames](https://www.londonyouthrowing.com/Event/racethethames2021).

This is part 1 of 3 of the interview. Enjoy!
