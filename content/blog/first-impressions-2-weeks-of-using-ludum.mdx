The Royal Shrewsbury School Senior Girls Rowing Coach, Will Reynolds, has been [using Ludum to speed up the planning and communication](https://ludum.com/blog/coaching/how-oxford-university-lightweight-rowing-club-use-ludum/) for his team. The Ludum platform and App has saved hours of admin per week. Will no longer sends out 7 changed training schedules each week but is able to [plan and notify of sessions ahead of time via the mobile app](https://ludum.com/blog/coaching/ludum-coaching-app-can-help-manage-your-club/). Will set crews, tweaks attendance and instantly notifies his squads, meaning his athletes are able to better optimise their training, upload their own data and take more ownership.

The Royal Shrewsbury School Boat club is nationally recognised as one of the leading rowing schools in the country and boasts an impressive 14 Henley Royal Regatta wins to its name.
