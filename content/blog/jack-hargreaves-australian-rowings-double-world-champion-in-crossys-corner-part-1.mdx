A rising star in Australian Rowing, Jack Hargreaves channelled the disappointment of missing the Rio Olympic Games in 2016 into becoming a back to back World Champion Rower in 2017 and 2018 in the outstanding Australian Men’s Coxless Four. Often referenced by the rowing community as the flagship crew for the recent shift toward higher stroke rates, Jack’s four has been discussed in several editions of Crossy’s Corner before.

In this interview Jack discusses his training methods, reveals Erg times, opens up about how he has dealt with the cancellation of the Olympics, and more…
