By connecting Float to your [Ludum](https://ludum.com/blog/data-performance-analytics/what-is-ludum-using-data-to-improve-your-sporting-performance/) account you will never have to type your results in ever again.

The most modern and interactive Rowing Machine App. Giving you access to information you do not even get on the display. Keep an eye on your stroke length during your Rowing Machine Workout.

Float records all the training you do on the Concept2 Indoor Rowing machine, and gives you more data during your workout and greater analysis after your workout.
