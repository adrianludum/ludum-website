All aspiring Olympic athletes are battling with the social impact of Coronavirus but three time World Rowing Champion and Boat Race winner Olivia Coffey has had to take time away from training to focus on battling Covid-19 herself.

In this interview Martin finds out how it’s impacted her rowing training, learned some more about her experience at Cambridge University and discusses how soon training will resume in the United States.
