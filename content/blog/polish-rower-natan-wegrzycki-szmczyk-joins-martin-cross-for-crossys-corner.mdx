Natan has been consistently working through the single sculling ranks culminating with a Silver Medal at the recent European Championships (2020) ahead of Kjetil Borch and squeezing the much fancied Oli Zeidler out of the medals.

In addition he’s an alum of Cal where he rowed under Mike Teti and stroked the 2019 Cambridge Blue boat to victory rowing with GB Rowing legends James Cracknell and Dave Bell.
