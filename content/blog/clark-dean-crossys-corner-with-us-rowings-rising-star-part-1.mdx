Clark Dean may be a new name to many rowing fans, but even though he’s only 20 he’s ready made quite a mark on the world rowing scene winning his first Junior Worlds medal at the age of 16 in the 4x before winning the 1x back to back in 2017 and 2018 setting a worlds fastest time of [6:45](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G67sMbAPDDM&t=405s) in the process, only 1s outside of the U23 record. 

This is the first part of three videos, there’s a link to the next video at the end of each one.
