Polish Rower Marcin Brzeziński is a veteran on the international racing scene with medals in both sculling and rowing.

Most recently Marcin and his crew won the Coxless Fours event at the 2019 World Championships and were understandably disappointed by the need to postpone the 2020 Olympics due to the pandemic.

This interview was broadcast live on Thursday 11th February 2021 and is the first part of three. Enjoy!
