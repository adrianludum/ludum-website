John Wojtkiewicz is a Coach and Consultant who caused a stir on LinkedIn with his series of blog posts entitled “[Rowing as a Zero-Billion Dollar Industry](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/rowing-zero-billion-dollar-industry-how-we-devalue-our-wojtkiewicz/)” debunking the myth that there’s no money in rowing and making his case for the possibility that maybe we’re just doing it wrong… 

In this interview Martin Cross discusses with John some of his ideas around how rowing needs to change it’s ways in order to move beyond it’s penny pinching ways to offer a better experience and in turn reap the rewards. 

This is the first video in a series of three. Enjoy!
