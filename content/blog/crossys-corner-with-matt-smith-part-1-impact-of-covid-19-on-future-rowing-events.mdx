Matt Smith is the Executive Director of World Rowing and in this interview he and Martin Cross discuss the future of rowing not just in the context of the [Covid-19 pandemic](https://ludum.com/blog/coaching/coaching-through-covid-round-table-part-1/) but also it’s inclusion in the Olympics and what format changes are being looked at.

For fans of the sport, it’s a really topical discussion and we’re really grateful to Matt for taking the time to talk to Martin and for being so open.
