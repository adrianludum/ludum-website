Georgie Rowe is an Australian Rower who has burst onto the international scene in recent years as part of the successful Australian Women’s Eight but also thanks to her prowess on the Concept 2 Rowing Machine where she’s made a habit of smashing records… During the coronavirus lockdown she’s broken 4 records for fun!

Georgie’s a breathe of fresh air and we’re certain that you’ll enjoy this interview!
