Dutch Rowing star Ruben Knab joins Martin Cross in this edition of Crossy’s Corner.

Filmed in the week before the Dutch Men’s 8+ is due to take on the German Mens 8+ (DeutschlandAchter) at the European Championships Martin and Ruben cover topics such as the perks of Red Bull sponsorship, the challenges posed by the postponement of the Olympics, the excitement of racing resuming and why Ruben’s role model is a fictional character!!! (can you guess who it is?)

This interview is split into three sections, this is the first part of the three. Enjoy!
