Anna Watkins was a key part of the GB Team’s success at the London 2012 Olympics; winning the women’s double with Dame Katherine Grainger. 

Starting as a college rower Anna was fast tracked through the World Class Start program and went from strength to strength representing GB at two Olympics, pulling some impressive Indoor Rowing scores along the way!
