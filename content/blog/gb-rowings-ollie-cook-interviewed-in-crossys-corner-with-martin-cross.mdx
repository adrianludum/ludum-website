World Champion and Boat Race Winner Ollie Cook joins Martin Cross for this interview just as the UK moves into a second Covid-19 lockdown. 

In this interview he and Martin discuss the vacuum left by Jurgen’s departure, the possibility of Jurgen’s return, some of Ollie’s experiences of racing, being an Olympic spare as well as mindfulness and lots more…

This is the first part of three. The link to the next video is at the end.
