You could be forgiven for thinking that Matt Gotrel switched from Rowing to Sailing, but he actually switched from Sailing to Rowing (won an Olympic Gold Medal in the 2016 GB 8+) and then switched back!

This is a fascinating interview giving an insight into the peculiar world of the America’s Cup; individualised training on an unprecedented level, constantly changing rules and spying on your rivals being part of the game! It is also interesting to hear how this compares to Matt’s time at Caversham Lake and the Great Britain Rowing Team High Performance Programme.
