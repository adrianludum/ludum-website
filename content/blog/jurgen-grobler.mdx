We went LIVE with Jürgen on Sunday 24 January as part of the virtual event, the Rowers Conference 2021.

This year we brought you Jürgen Grobler’s first public interview since retiring from British Rowing in August 2020.

Martin Cross OLY interviewed Jürgen for his first in-depth interview about his coaching career.

Listen to Martin Cross OLY and Jürgen Grobler discuss coaching philosophy, success and hear one of the world’s greatest coaches share his insights into what makes athletes win… Enjoy!

### Like Jurgen, you can take your athletes to the next level. Write stronger training plans and make coaching decisions based on powerful analytics.
