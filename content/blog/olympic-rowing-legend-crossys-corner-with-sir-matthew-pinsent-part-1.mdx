Sir Matthew Pinsent is universally known throughout the rowing community, both as an outstanding Olympic Athlete with four Gold Medals from consecutive Olympics, but also as a broadcaster and Steward of Henley Royal Regatta. 

Martin catches up with Matthew during lockdown to discuss highlights of Matthew’s rowing career as well as getting all the latest HRR gossip as the regatta announces it’s plans for the 2020 Regatta.
