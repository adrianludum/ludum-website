How do you continue to [coach your team during a global pandemic](https://ludum.com/blog/coaching/olympic-athletes-and-coaches-training-in-isolation/) and lockdown measures?

We assembled a group of coaches from around the world to discuss their experience of lockdown, share what’s worked and what hasn’t and try to get a feel for how we, as a sport, might start to transition out of lockdown measures and back to normality.

Andrew Carter – Women’s Rowing Coach at the University of Iowa
 Kieran Clark – Lead Coach at Tideway Scullers School
 Dave Currie – Director of Rowing at Shiplake College
 Roger Barrow – National Coach – Rowing South Africa
